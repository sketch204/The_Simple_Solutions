What I have done in the past

In the past I have created many different projects. Some I am very proud of. Others I wonder how could I have ever come up with something so ridiculous. Almost half of the projects that I have done are academic, including a Merge Sort algorithm, and Binary Search algorithm and the biggest one a java based educational game. The game is called Rearing Ranch. It is a very simple game that I made with a friend of mine that is targeted towards kids from age 3-5 and teaches them simple colours, animals and numbers.

In my spare time I have also developed, and continuing to work on, a school app that I have developed with that same friend. The app is actually finished and is available on the app store and an android version should be finished soon. The app is called Lyon’s Den. It is supposed to help the student and the teacher body to connect with the school’s community through announcements and quick access to school events through the calendar. It offers other features as well such as the schedule for the day and a list of teachers for contact information.
